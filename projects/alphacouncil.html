AlphaCouncil: Analytical Investment Decision Tool

Multi-Agent LLMs · Python

Overview

A consensus-based analytical tool using 3 specialized AI agents (Quant, Fundamental, Risk) to support trade desks with data-driven insights and investment recommendations. AlphaCouncil synthesizes conflicting market data into actionable trade signals.

Key Features

  • 3 specialized agents: Quant, Fundamental, and Risk analysis
  • Synthesis of conflicting market data for 100+ tickers into actionable signals
  • Thematic research support for institutional-grade portfolios
  • Data-driven investment recommendations for trade desks

Technologies

Python, Streamlit, LangChain, Large Language Models (LLMs)
